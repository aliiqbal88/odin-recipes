Milkshake

Description

A tasty milkshake for summer days that is really easy to make

Ingredients

  • Milk: 1 cup
  • Sugar: 2 tbsp.
  • Bananas: 4 no.

Steps

  1. Pour milk in a grinder.
  2. Add sugar and bananas to it.
  3. Close the lid of the jug and turn the grinder on.
  4. Keep the grinder running for 4 minutes. After which pour your milkshake into a jug.
  5. Enjoy
