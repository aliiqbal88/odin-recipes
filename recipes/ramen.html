Ramen

Description

A cheap, easy, tasty and quick solution to your emergency hunger cravings

Ingredients

  • Ramen: 1 pack
  • Cheese: 3 slices
  • Water: 2 cups
  • thyme: As desired

Steps

    Fill a pan with excess water and bring it to boil
    Once the water is boiling, put in the ramen. Turn the heat low and let it simmer for 2 minutes
    After 2 minutes, put in the spices and throughly mix.
    Add the cheese slices while still mixing.
    place the now cooked ramen in to a plate and garnish with thyme
    Enjoy the tasty meal.
