Corn Soup

Description

A nice and healthy way to stay hydrated and full

Ingredients

  • Chicken (boneless): 1 kg
  • Water: 1 ltr.
  • Sweet Corns: 1 can
  • Salt: 0.5 teaspoon
  • eggs (whites only): 2

Steps

  1. Pour the water into a pot and bring it to boil
  2. Add salt and chicken. Let it cook for 10 minutes
  3. Add the can of sweet corns. Turn down the heat and let it simmer for further 10 minutes
  4. Slowly and carefully add the egg whites to the broth. Make sure you do it slowly while stirring the pot.
  5. Turn off the heat and your delicous soup is ready
